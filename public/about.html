Q: What is Book-a-Book?

Books are stupid expensive, but information and knowledge should be free. Or at least just paid for once. To that end, we created Book-a-Book so that those sexy books you've finished reading don't have to keep all their sexy info to themselves. Pay your books forward.

Q: Who built it?

Book-a-Book was built as part of a 2-day hackathon for Elevation Academy, a Tel Aviv-based programming bootcamp. It was built by Alex & Shai, who rock.
